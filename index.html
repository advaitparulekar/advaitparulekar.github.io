Advait Parulekar

I am a fifth year PhD student advised by Prof. Sanjay Shakkottai in the Wireless Networking and Communications Group at UT Austin. Before joining UT Austin, I received my B.S. in Computer Science from Texas A&M University.

I work on statistical learning theory, most recently on problems related to learning good representations for data. I have also worked on some Bandit and RL problems.
